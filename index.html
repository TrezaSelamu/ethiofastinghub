Discover Our Fasting Services

Our fasting programs are designed to fit your personal health goals and lifestyle. Explore our offerings to find the perfect plan for you.

  • Intermittent Fasting (IF)

    Description: This is an umbrella term for various fasting schedules. The most common is the 16:8 method, where you fast for 16 hours and eat within an 8-hour window.

    Benefits: Helps with weight loss, improves metabolic health, and is relatively easy to incorporate into daily life.

    Challenges: Adjusting to the fasting period can be difficult initially.

    Estimated Weight Loss: 2-4 kg per month.

    Learn More

  • Extended Fasting

    Description: Involves fasting for more than 24 hours, often 48-72 hours or even longer.

    Benefits: Can lead to significant weight loss and autophagy (cellular repair).

    Challenges: Very challenging and should be done under medical supervision.

    Estimated Weight Loss:5-7 kg per month (if done periodically).

    Learn More

  • Water Fasting

    Description: Involves consuming only water for a set period, typically 24-72 hours.

    Benefits: Can lead to rapid weight loss and detoxification.

    Challenges: Can be very challenging and should be done under medical supervision, especially for extended periods.

    Estimated Weight Loss: 4-6 kg per month (if done periodically).

    Learn More

  • Alternate-Day Fasting (ADF)

    Description: This method involves fasting every other day. On fasting days, you either consume very few calories (about 500 calories) or none at all. On non-fasting days, you eat normally.

    Benefits: Can lead to significant weight loss and improved metabolic health.

    Challenges: May be difficult to maintain long-term due to the strict fasting days.

    Estimated Weight Loss: 3-5 kg per month.

    Learn More

  • OMAD (One Meal a Day)

    Description: You eat one large meal per day and fast for the remaining 23 hours.

    Benefits: Simplifies meal planning and can lead to significant calorie reduction.

    Challenges: May be difficult to get all necessary nutrients in one meal and can be hard to maintain.

    Estimated Weight Loss: 3-5 kg per month.

    Learn More

  • Warrior Diet

    Description: Involves eating small amounts of raw fruits and vegetables during the day and having one large meal at night, typically within a 4-hour eating window.

    Benefits: Can improve energy levels and promote weight loss.

    Challenges: The eating schedule can be difficult to adjust to and may not provide enough nutrients if not well-planned.

    Estimated Weight Loss: 2-4 kg per month.

    Learn More
