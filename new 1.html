Ethio Fasting Hub
Choose your fasting method to begin your wellness journey.
Intermittent Fasting
Cycles between eating and fasting within a set window (e.g., 16/8). Improves metabolism and balances insulin levels.
Extended Fasting
Fasting for longer than 24 hours, often lasting 48, 72 hours or more. Great for detox, fat loss, and autophagy.
OMAD (One Meal A Day)
Eating just one meal per day within a 1-hour window. Known for its simplicity and efficiency in boosting fat loss.
Water Fasting
A fasting method where only water is consumed. Promotes detox, autophagy, and fat loss.
Warrior Fasting
Eat small amounts of raw fruit and vegetables during the day, and one large meal at night. Focuses on primal eating patterns.
Alternate Day Fasting
Alternate between days of eating normally and fasting days with minimal calories. Known for boosting weight loss and metabolism.
